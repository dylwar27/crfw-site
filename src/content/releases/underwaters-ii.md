# underwaters II

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/underwaters]] — Vol. II  
**Release year:** ~2011 (exact date unconfirmed)  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/underwaters II`  
**Tracks:** 9  

The second volume expands the cast and dials up the absurdist register. *Nyquil gator dreams* is the standout title — gator meets pharmaceutical brand name, dreams used as a verb almost. *Lusty crawfish fuck hard* and *robo turtles party* push the naming into more explicitly energetic territory than Vol. I. The "robo" prefix (robo turtles, robo whale) introduces a mechanical dimension to the underwater ecosystem that persists through the series.

*Doll fins* is a pun on "dolphins" — the kind of small sonic joke embedded in a tracklist that rewards close reading.
