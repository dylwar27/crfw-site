# sep09

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/album-a-month]] — Month 9  
**Release date:** 2009-09-27  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/sep09`  
**Tracks:** 9  
**Format:** MP3 — first MP3-format album in the archive  

The moment the format changes. sep09 is the first alphabets album in MP3, signaling a production shift from GarageBand/iPhone M4A output to dedicated audio software. The gap between June and September 2009 (no July or August album in the archive) may reflect the transition period.

The titles here are the direct ancestors of the underwaters series: *giant amphibian's island noise*, *turtle games shell bonks*, *plesiosaur howl outs and swim bump*, *jive shark and lulla fish*. The aquatic-animal vocabulary that defines the underwaters series is fully formed here — Colin had been circling this territory since "getting astounded by blue whales on videos" in JANFEB09, and by September 2009 it's his primary naming register.

*autumn cub bites ferocious* opens the album with season — autumn, a fitting opener for September. *night explodes* and *seasons moves* are more abstract, less animal-specific. *whatever face swamp walk* is a complete absurdist sentence: "whatever face" (dismissive expression as a noun phrase) + "swamp walk" (a specific gait through specific terrain).
