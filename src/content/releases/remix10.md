Second annual alphabets remix compilation. Released December 27, 2010 as a 24-track digital release on Bandcamp. The album follows REMIX09 (December 2009) as part of an annual remix series. Tags on Bandcamp: electronic, alien, experimental, noise, rhythm, tropical, Denver. Bandcamp announced by Westword in December 2010 as a pay-what-you-want download.

Colin remixed 24 artists' tracks. Several artists (Modern Witch, Thundercade, Married in Berdichev, Swimming With Seaturtles) also appeared on REMIX09, confirming sustained collaborative relationships. Track 7's mix name — "cave rave mix" — predates the CAVE RAVE album (2014) by four years and may be an early use of that phrase.

## Scene notes

Artists continuing from REMIX09: Hideous Men (track 7), Modern Witch (track 8), Swimming With Seaturtles (track 20), Married in Berdichev (track 23). Track 24 — Pollination Population — is Katherine Ersing's Denver experimental project (bells, cassette tape samples, Westword-featured). Stephen Steinbrink (track 3) is a San Francisco-based songwriter with Denver connections. Altar Eagle was a project by Alex Lambert (Denver). "NIGHT OF JOY" (track 16) is likely a Denver band.
