Second annual alphabets remix compilation. Released December 27, 2010 as a 24-track digital release on Bandcamp. The album follows REMIX09 (December 2009) as part of an annual remix series. Tags on Bandcamp: electronic, alien, experimental, noise, rhythm, tropical, Denver.

## Partial Tracklist (4 of 24 confirmed from Bandcamp track pages; full tracklist not yet documented)

1. MODERN WITCH-HOLLYWOOD BABYLON (nine inch witch mix)
2. THUNDERCADE-PERIOD OF THE FUNCTION (deep tattoo smooches mix)
3. CONSTELLATIONS-SAVAGE ORB (scary dipper mix)
4. CVLTS-RADD PITT (clavinova_mic2 mix)
5–24. _(not yet documented — needs full Bandcamp pull)_

Note: The 4 tracks above appear to be Colin's remix versions of other artists' tracks — the naming convention [ARTIST-TRACK (mix name)] suggests he was remixing collaborators or peers from the Denver/experimental scene. The named artists (Modern Witch, Thundercade, Constellations, CVLTS) are likely Denver-adjacent artists; further research warranted.
