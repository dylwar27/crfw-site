# Timewave686

**Project:** [[projects/killd-by]]  
**Status:** Unreleased  
**File date:** 2016-08-10  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/KB/killd by/Timewave686`  
**Tracks:** 8 (unmastered AIF files)  

An 8-track unreleased killd by project from August 2016. The name "Timewave686" may reference *Timewave Zero*, a numerological theory by Terence McKenna proposing that time has a wave-like structure of novelty — a concept circulating in psychedelic and counterculture communities. The "686" suffix is unresolved: it may be a frequency (686 Hz is within the red light spectrum), a code, or a personal reference.

Several track names in this folder overlap with *janefonda_beta-max* (a December 2015 folder), suggesting these tracks were developed across sessions over roughly 8 months before being grouped under the Timewave686 name:
- *captivity_-muviez* appears in both
- *jF_bmx1* and *jF_bmx2* appear in both (the "jF" prefix = "jane fonda")
- *burndownthezoo* appears in G2D2demos as well

This cross-folder recurrence is significant: it confirms Colin used a working method of developing tracks across multiple project folders before finalizing them under a release name.
