# underwaters III

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/underwaters]] — Vol. III  
**Release year:** ~2011 (exact date unconfirmed)  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/underwaters III`  
**Tracks:** 11  

The largest volume of the underwaters series and its emotional peak. Eleven tracks cover the widest range of the series: glam, violence (*pirate deaths*, *submarine deaths*), labor (*builder lobster workers*, *walk it crabs*), and finally, at the end — *sad whale*. 

The closing track title is different in kind from everything before it. Where the rest of the series deals in action and description — parties, pukes, growl swims — *sad whale* is purely emotional. The whale is just sad. No qualifier, no verb. It reads like a thesis statement for the whole project: at the bottom of the ocean, the largest creature is also the loneliest.

*Scuba bats* is a standout for combining an aerial animal with underwater equipment — bats, echolocators, now adapted for ocean depth. *Punkd out screamer dolphins* applies counter-cultural affect to the most sonically expressive marine mammals.
