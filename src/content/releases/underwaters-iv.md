# underwaters IV

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/underwaters]] — Vol. IV  
**Release year:** ~2012 (exact date unconfirmed)  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/underwaters IV`  
**Tracks:** 10  

Volume IV is the darkest and most ceremonial installment. Where earlier volumes were playful and frenetic, IV descends: *ancient tortoise chants low* opens with geological time; *otter shaman dark magic potions* introduces ritual; *deepsea pegasus armies* brings mythological creatures to the ocean floor. The "deepsea" prefix (used twice) signals the series going further down than before.

*Scubaboon* — a portmanteau of scuba and baboon — is one of the more surreally funny titles in the entire catalog. *Ribbon raptors* closes the volume with speed and violence, a contrast to the slow-opening ancient tortoise.

*R lab* is the most cryptic title in the series: no creature, no description, just an abbreviation. May refer to a location, a process, or be a deliberate non sequitur.
