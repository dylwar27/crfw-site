# octobrr09

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/album-a-month]] — Month 10  
**Release date:** October 2009  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/octobrr09`  
**Tracks:** 11  
**Format:** MP3  

The first "octobrr" — the spelling with double R that Colin would repeat in OCTOBRR2010, marking October as a recurring touchstone. The extra R in "brr" makes it cold, onomatopoetic, seasonal. October with a shiver built into its name.

11 tracks in the warmer-cooler register of autumn. The titles shift slightly from the pure animal vocabulary of sep09 toward more emotional/sensory terrain: *bouncy places*, *dreamy new people*, *giggle places*, *talkless places*, *band aids heal places*. "Places" recurs four times — a spatial vocabulary of feeling. *heart throb blast ups* and *romantipunk* establish a strand of romance-adjacent language (compare *romantidrunk* in SUNPOWER and *romantibunk* in OCTOBRR2010).

*sftwre synths float tense* is the most self-referential title — "software synths" written in the compressed abbreviated style that would later name the December companion release SFTWRSYNTHZdec09. *frost paws pause* closes the album: the season arriving.

## Archive Notes

- "octobrr" spelling: double-R appears again in OCTOBRR2010; a deliberate seasonal orthography
- "places" as a recurring noun: *bouncy places*, *giggle places*, *talkless places*, *band aids heal places* — four tracks using the same word, creating a loose suite within the album
- *romantipunk* echoes forward to *romantidrunk* (SUNPOWER) and *romantibunk* (OCTOBRR2010) — a recurring portmanteau family
