A single track made *for* someone: the folder name reads "for janie chant dance" — a dedication, an instruction, a gift. The track is titled *genie roberts*.

**Jane Roberts** (1929–1984) was an American author and psychic medium best known for channeling "Seth," a non-physical entity, and transcribing his teachings in the *Seth Material* books (*Seth Speaks*, *The Nature of Personal Reality*, etc.). Roberts was influential in New Age, metaphysical, and consciousness communities. "Genie roberts" may be a play on "Jane Roberts" — phonetic near-rhyme — positioning the track as a spirit channeling of sorts, or a tribute to her work.

The folder name "for janie" (using the diminutive "Janie") suggests this was made for a specific person named Janie (or Jane) as a chant/dance piece. Whether Janie was someone in Colin's life who appreciated the Seth Material, or whether the pun on Jane Roberts was the entire point, is unresolved.

One track, made for someone, on February 4, 2011 — three days after the JANFEB11 PIRATE LIFE files were completed. Both appeared in the same two-month window.
