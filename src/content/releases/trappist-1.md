Colin created this track approximately two months after that announcement, naming the project after the newly-discovered system.

The TRAPPIST-1 announcement was culturally significant in early 2017 — widely shared as evidence of potentially habitable worlds. Colin's naming of this project reflects the same pattern visible elsewhere in the killd by era: referencing news and scientific discovery as titles (*trappist-1*, *Timewave686*), grounding abstract sonic work in contemporary cultural moments.
