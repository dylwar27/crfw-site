Loose "unreleased" container — not a coherent release, but a late-career
folder-of-loose-ends that Colin himself appears to have curated. The two
tracks span October 2010 (peak BIEBER JERBOA / CAVE RAVE-adjacent era) and
February 2013 (near the tail of the Alphabets cadence), so this is not a
session remnant — it's a bin of keep-worthy orphans from across the
Alphabets run.

## Confirmed tracks (from archive filenames)

- bip bop.mp3 _(mtime 2010-10-30, 5.09 MB)_
- lust howls.mp3 _(mtime 2013-02-19, 8.10 MB; .asd sidecar 2015-09-15)_

## Notes

The .asd sidecar on `lust howls.mp3` means Colin had that track loaded in
Ableton as late as September 2015 — possibly reworking it, possibly just
auditioning it. Worth looking at whether any 2015-era release (or the later
Killd By material) draws on `lust howls` thematically.

The container naming (plain `unreleased`) distinguishes this from all the
period-specific "unreleased X" folders — `unreleased fall 08`,
`unreleased_dec08` patterns, etc. Those are per-session; this is a curated
overflow.
