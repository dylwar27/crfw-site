# JUNE_MONSTERS_09

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/album-a-month]] — Month 6  
**Release date:** June 2009  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/JUNE_MONSTERS_09`  
**Tracks:** 15  
**Format:** M4A  

The first fully themed album in the alphabets catalog. Every track follows the format: **[adjective/type] monster(s) [action/description]**. No exceptions. June 2009 was when Colin locked into theme as an organizing principle — a practice he'd develop through the underwaters series, the POWER family, and ultimately through JANFEB11 PIRATE LIFE.

The monsters here are elemental: sunlight monsters, glitter monsters, garden monsters, tribal monsters, cave monsters, mud monsters. Each pair of words creates a small scene — "mud monsters night swamp sludge ballad" is a genre description and a movie title and a song description all at once.

"rory dog monster sleeps forever" closes the album with a name — Rory — which makes this track personal in a way the others aren't. Whether Rory was a dog, a person, a character, or something else is unresolved.

Track 09 appears twice in the archive (both "power monsters work out fitness" and "glam monsters shift the pounce"), suggesting a revision or version conflict. Both tracks are included in the count.
