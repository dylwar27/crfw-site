Spring–summer 2008 folder with a nature-themed title. Notably contains a track named "freak medicine.m4a" which suggests a thematic or literal continuation from the "freak medicine winter 07" folder. Mixed format (M4A and MP3) indicating this spans the transition period.

## Confirmed tracks (from archive filenames)

- 01 active hours.m4a
- 02 baby windchimes in the mouths.m4a
- 02 believe in you,us,everyone.mp3
- 04 i want to kiss like megaman can.mp3
- 06 freak medicine.m4a ← note: same phrase as earlier folder title
- 06 periwinkle breakfasts.m4a
- 06 some sort of cool looking bird trapped in water and yelling for help.m4a
- 07 got sick, had fun.m4a
- 07 starving mammal video life.mp3
- _(additional tracks; full count not yet confirmed)_

## Notes

Track number collisions (multiple track 06, multiple track 07) suggest either a multi-session folder or tracks from different recording days merged together. "i want to kiss like megaman can" is an early example of video-game cultural reference in the catalog. "some sort of cool looking bird trapped in water and yelling for help" is among the longer and more evocative track titles from this era.
