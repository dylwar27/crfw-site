# SUNPOWER2

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/power]] — [___]POWER family / SUNPOWER Vol. 2  
**File date:** 2010-06-15  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/SUNPOWER2`  
**Tracks:** 6  

The second SUNPOWER volume, released two weeks after the first. The track naming here is looser and more pop-cultural than Vol. 1: *enya got punkd* references both the musician Enya and the MTV show Punk'd — an image of new-age ambient pop being pranked. *Power hunger* as a closing track mirrors the series title — a reflexive gesture, making the theme of "power" the subject of the final song.

*Rooster* and *other birds* continue a subtle ornithological streak that runs through the POWER family (SUNPOWER's *hoops* has avian associations; BELLPOWER's *two* recalls bird calls in some readings). *2 clock* echoes *three clock* from Vol. 1 — Colin noting the time again, slightly earlier in the night.
