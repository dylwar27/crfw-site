## Notes

remnants in adjacent years should be checked — they may form a pattern of
"only the take Colin liked from a multi-track session was kept under its own
folder." If a `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/` sweep surfaces other
single-file folders with a high track number, they likely belong to the same
class.
