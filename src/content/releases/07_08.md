## Notes

Track titles suggest the characteristic Colin Ward style already present in 2007–2008: animals, body experiences, and abstract imagery ("baby mountain," "J.J. Salamander," "Techniques and Materials"). The `.mp3` files in this folder are unusual for this period — most early recordings are M4A.
