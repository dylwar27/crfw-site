# underwaters V

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/underwaters]] — Vol. V  
**Release year:** ~2012–2013 (exact date unconfirmed)  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/underwaters V`  
**Tracks:** 6  

The most compact completed volume of the series. Six tracks, sparse, and closing with *sea sick* — a word that refers both to nausea from ocean motion and a particular longing for the sea. As the penultimate completed volume (VI exists only as work files), V may function as a winding-down: fewer creatures, shorter titles, and a closing track that could be read as exhaustion or departure.

*Bubbles and vitamins* opens the volume with something almost domestic — both are things you encounter in a medicine cabinet or a fish tank. *Seacow beauty contest* is characteristically absurdist. *Giant poison beach lizards* pushes the ecosystem onto shore — a liminal creature, neither fully underwater nor fully land-based.

*Deep creak speak* is the most textural title of the series: creak as sound, deep as depth, speak as language — an image of the ocean communicating through structural groans.
