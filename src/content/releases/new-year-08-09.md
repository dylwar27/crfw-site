New Year's 2008–2009 recording folder. Five tracks, all M4A. Marks the transition point between the 2008 archive batch and the 2009 album-a-month series (which begins with JANFEB09). The track "new year.m4a" is likely the title piece.

## Tracklist (from archive filenames)

- c.m4a
- holiday suitcase.m4a
- new year.m4a
- s.m4a
- you and the light train.m4a

## Notes

Single-letter track names "c.m4a" and "s.m4a" are unusual — possibly sketches, interludes, or placeholder names. "holiday suitcase" and "you and the light train" suggest travel/holiday imagery consistent with the year-end timing. "you and the light train" is an evocative title that anticipates the more lyrical track naming in the 2009+ releases.
