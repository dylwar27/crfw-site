Mid-2008, 5 tracks. Notable as one of the earliest folders explicitly labeled as an "EP," showing Colin already applying EP-format thinking by mid-2008. Mixed M4A/MP3 format.

## Tracklist (from archive filenames)

1. 1 powerups with geese.m4a
2. 2 new age guy.m4a
3. 3 play nice.m4a
4. 4 magic knives at the equator.m4a
5. 5 dumb gull.mp3

## Notes

The title track "dumb gull" (track 5) is an MP3 where the others are M4A, possibly indicating the title track was a different session or post-processed. "powerups with geese" combines video-game terminology ("powerups") with animal imagery in a manner that prefigures later animal-digital mashups. "new age guy" may be a self-referential or ironic label. The naming convention with numbered prefixes (1, 2, 3...) appears here early, continuing through later releases.
