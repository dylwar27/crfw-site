The third and final SUNPOWER volume, arriving over a year after the first two (which were released weeks apart in summer 2010). This gap is significant — it places SUNPOWER3 in the middle of Colin's most prolific alphabets period rather than alongside its predecessors. The tone shifts accordingly: 5 tracks vs. 6, and titles that feel more geographic and textural.

*Manajunk n floody* references Manayunk, a neighborhood in Philadelphia, suggesting either travel or a specific reference to a place or person. *Sunkissed goose* brings back the avian quality of the SUNPOWER family. *Puma* — one word, a predatory cat — closes the volume with compressed intensity.
