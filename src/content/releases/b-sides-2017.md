Unreleased 2017 b-sides collection, distinct from the released *2014-2016 [Bsides]* Bandcamp album. Four tracks confirmed from AIF files dated June 8, 2017. A fifth file — *penguin2.wav* (dated 2005) — is likely a sample rather than a track: its 2005 date predates the killd by project by nearly a decade.

Track naming follows killd by conventions: abbreviated/compressed words (*nonimo*, *swoo107*, *trntla114*) alongside a more direct title (*tsunami*). The "107" in *swoo107* and "114" in *trntla114* appear to be BPM or tempo markers embedded in working filenames — consistent with *showmeLuvbassline127* in Timewave686.

*swoo* appears elsewhere in the archive as *chi_swoo_*, one of Colin's Bandcamp aliases. *trntla* may abbreviate "Toronto, LA" or be another personal code.
