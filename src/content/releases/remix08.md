# REMIX08

The earliest entry in the REMIX series. Archive-fragmentary — only one of
what was almost certainly a larger tracklist survives locally.

## Confirmed tracks (from archive filenames)

- `14 L3GOMUSEUM 3D.m4a` _(file mtime 2008-11-07; track position 14)_

## Notes

The `L3GOMUSEUM 3D` title follows the REMIX-series convention Colin's other
compilations use: `[ORIGINAL ARTIST]-[ORIGINAL TRACK] ([colin's mix name] mix)`
— but here the "(mix)" suffix is missing and the original-artist prefix is
unclear, so the filename may be a shorthand or a work-in-progress title
rather than the compilation's finalized track name. "L3GO MUSEUM 3D" reads
like a Colin coinage (LEGO + MUSEUM + 3D, in his usual absurdist-collision
style), which would mean this is a *self*-remix rather than a remix of
another artist — possible given that later REMIX releases include self-remix
entries (Snake Feathers on REMIX09, per `series/remix.md`).

## Research gaps

- Original artist (if any) unidentified.
- Bandcamp page not located — either non-existent or not public.
- Tracks 1–13 and 15+ are not in the archive. They may exist in other
  alphabets session folders from 2008 under different names (per Colin's
  pattern of pre-compilation session staging), or they may be lost.
- Release date inferred from the "08" in the title; no confirmation.

## Relationship to fragmentary class

With this entry, the known fragmentary-remnant folders in the Alphabets
archive are:

- `unreleased fall 08` — track 04 only (Session 10)
- `pears092010` — track 04 only (Sept 2010)
- `DDR2_HAUNTED` — track 02 only (May 2012; also has a stray .asd sidecar for track 06)
- **REMIX08 — track 14 only (Nov 2008)**

The pattern appears validated: across 2008–2012, several alphabets session
folders survive as the singular "keep" take from a larger multi-track
session. A systematic `fragmentary` tag across all four would let future
passes treat them as a class.
