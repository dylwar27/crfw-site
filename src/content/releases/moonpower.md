# moonpower

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/power]] — [___]POWER family (chronologically first)  
**File date:** 2010-05-07  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/moonpower`  
**Tracks:** 10  

The chronologically first release in the [___]POWER family (May 7, 2010 — predating SUNPOWER by 23 days), and the largest by track count (10 tracks). Note that the archive folder is lowercase *moonpower* while SUNPOWER, BELLPOWER, and DANCEPOWER are uppercase — possibly a naming evolution or an intentional distinction.

moonpower has the most evocative tracklist in the family: *sexy owls* (nocturnal creatures, rendered glamorous), *rapid eye portals* (sleep/REM as architecture), *pillow dizzies* (half-awake sensation), *whoahowwow* (onomatopoeia/exclamation as title). This is nighttime music — the moon as power source rather than the sun.

*Laugh necks* and *core eh doors* are characteristically cryptic. *Hey may* may be a vocative — addressing May (the month? a person?). *Rooms* is the simplest and most ambient-sounding title, a single noun.

*Crazy* closes the set — a word so common it feels like a wink, after nine titles of elaborate absurdism.
