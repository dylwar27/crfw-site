# JULY2010

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]

A summer album with gaps — the track numbering runs up to 12 but only 9 tracks are in the archive (01, 02, 04 not present; 03, 05-09, 11-12 confirmed). The missing tracks may have been removed or not preserved. File dates (August 2, 2010) show slight overrun past July.

**"toby in hawaii"** — Toby appears here, in Hawaii, on a summer album. Toby previously appeared in *toby dance* (DANCEPOWER, July 8, 2010), just weeks earlier. Two July 2010 appearances of Toby. Whether Toby is a real person, a character, or a recurring invented figure is unresolved; the specificity suggests a real person.

**"tekken2"** — a direct video game reference (Tekken 2 was released in 1996). Gaming references are relatively rare in the alphabets catalog, making this notable.

"sharky shirt" and "all thunder stormy" are quintessentially summer titles. "lemonade" (track 09) is one of the simplest track names in the entire catalog — a single noun, season-appropriate. "fair uh moans" sounds like *pheromones* spoken aloud, phonetically transcribed.
