# APRIL CO-EDS 2011

**Project:** [[projects/alphabets]]  
**Series:** [[series/album-a-month]] — April 2011  
**File dates:** 2011-05-04 (slight overrun from April)  
**Archive location:** `_Documentation/Music/alphabets/APRIL CO-EDS 2011`  
**Tracks:** 6  
**Format:** MP3  

Six tracks for April 2011. The title "CO-EDS" carries collegiate or communal associations — co-educational, people learning together, a group. Spring 2011, a compact album after the bigger JANFEB11 PIRATE LIFE.

**"fantasia2011.mp3"** (track 05) — the earliest archive evidence of a Fantasia event specifically in 2011. Combined with "run it run it fantasia mixx" from NOV09, this confirms Fantasia was a recurring event (not a one-time occurrence) predating the first vaulted Fantasia (2012) by at least two years and the concept itself dating to November 2009.

"903 bliss mist" (track 04) — the number 903 is unresolved; could be an address, a frequency, a date, or a personal code. "Bliss" connects to "bliss body" from NOV09.

"big poodle sharp tooth" (track 06) closes the album with an animal image — the poodle (domestic, groomed, unexpected) given a sharp tooth (wild, predatory).

"magi lash" (track 01) — "magi" as in the three wise men, or magic? "Lash" as a whip strike or an eyelash? The compression is deliberate.

## Archive Notes

- File dates: 2011-05-04 — slightly past April
- **"fantasia2011"** confirms Fantasia 2011 as a real event; predates the vaulted Fantasia 2012 by one year
- This is the last multi-track album in the album-a-month series before the project winds down to a single track (feb2012)
- "bliss mist" in track 04: the word "bliss" appears across the monthly catalog (NOV09: "bliss body"; APRIL 2011: "903 bliss mist") — a recurring word with personal significance
