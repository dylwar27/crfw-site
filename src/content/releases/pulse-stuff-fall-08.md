## Notes

Track number collisions (two track 03s) suggest multi-session folder. "huge antenna, empathic stuff" is a characteristically long descriptive title. "lets try it again, shell.." is an unusually personal/conversational title. "moved the same" has an abstract, emotional quality unlike the more playful animal-themed titles from earlier in 2008 — possibly evidence of a stylistic shift in fall 2008.
