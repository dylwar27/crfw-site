May 2010 — the same month that moonpower (May 7) and SUNPOWER (May 30) were released. This was Colin's single most prolific calendar month, with at least three releases (may10, moonpower, SUNPOWER) and possibly more. The album-a-month project and the [___]POWER family were running simultaneously.

The may10 titles are warmer and more open than the winter 2009 albums: *cloudy*, *friend ships*, *light talk*, *make those plans*, *sweat*. Spring vocabulary. *friend ships* (as two words — friendship rendered as vessels) and *sad trains* sit alongside the warmer titles, keeping the emotional range honest.

*hott* — a single word, one of the simplest titles in the catalog — would echo in the later *stupid HOTT* Bandcamp release. *funky weather stuff* is a quintessentially alphabets title: a vague modifier (*funky*), a natural phenomenon (*weather*), and a vague noun (*stuff*).
