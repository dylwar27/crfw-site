Posthumous compilation released April 3, 2020 by Noumenal Loom. Draws from material recorded 2014–2017 across the Killd By catalog. Limited to 100 cassettes. All proceeds benefit the CRFW Fund. Described in press as featuring "vocoded vox, featherweight synths, scattershot drums and icy timbres."
